You can upload video up to 200 MBytes in size. For larger files, compress them first:

  1. Using ffmpeg tool and H.264 codec:
    ffmpeg -i initial_video .mp4 -codec:a copy -vcodec libx264 -preset
    slow -crf 25 compressed_video .mp4
  2. Using Avidemux, free tool, also available for any platform.

See also Vimeo guidelines for best practices.
